A Few Trips Taken

Arizona-Utah
Arizona-Utah National Parks Circuit:
  • Flew into Las Vagas from Chicago; rented a passenger van.
  • Drove three hours to Williams, Arizona for the first night stay.
  • Took an old fashion train trip into Grand Canyon National Park.
  • Return to Williams, Arizona and the next day drove to Moab, Utah.
  • In the morning took a mountain biking excursion in Moab, Utah.
  • Hiked and drove through Arches National Park.
  • The morning of the next day drove through beautiful valleys to Bryce Canyon
  • Spent the night there to our final excursion near Zion, Utah
  • And back to the airport in Las Vegas.
